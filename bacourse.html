ADMISSION TO B.A

Admisshion-2024-25

B.A. (Bachelor of Arts) is a popular undergraduate degree program offered by universities and colleges in various disciplines related to arts, humanities, and social sciences. Here is a detailed overview of the B.A. course:

B.A. Course Details

  •   Duration:   Typically 3 years (6 semesters).
  • Eligibility:
  •   Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board.
  •   Minimum percentage required varies by institution (usually 40-50%).
  •   Mode:   Full-time, part-time, or distance education.
  •   Course Type:   General B.A. or specialized in a specific subject.

Common Specializations in B.A.

Sr. No. Languages Social Sciences Humanities Other Fields
1 Hindi, English, Urdu, Sanskrit, etc. History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Economics, Geography. Philosophy, Literature, Fine Arts. Journalism, Public Administration, Education.

B.A. Semester-1 Papers (Example Structure)

  • 1. Core Subject 1 (Major Subject - Paper 1)
  •   Example: Introduction to Political Theory, Ancient Indian History, or Foundations of Sociology.
  • 2. Core Subject 2 (Major Subject - Paper 2)
  •   Example: Example: Principles of Economics, General Psychology, or Physical Geography.
  • 3. Core Subject 3 (Optional/Elective - Paper 3)
  •   Example: Philosophy, Public Administration, or Fine Arts.
  • 4. Language Paper 1 (Compulsory Language)
  •   English Language: Focus on grammar, comprehension, and writing.
  •   FHindi Language or another regional language as applicable.

Paper Details (Papers for B.A. Semester-1)

Paper 1 Ancient Indian History (History)
Paper 2 Introduction to Political Theory (Political Science)
Paper 3 Foundations of Sociology (Sociology)
Paper 4 English Language (Prose, Poetry, and Grammar)
Paper 5 Environmental Studies (AECC)
Paper 6 (Optional) Hindi Language (if offered).

Skills Developed During B.A.

  •    Critical Thinking and Analysis
  •    Communication and Writing Skills
  •    Research and Problem Solving
  •    Cultural and Social Awareness
